Here’s a look at the tools I use the most when working on my various projects.

Adobe Creative Cloud for all my designing and creating of stuff. While I’m not a fan of their new subscription based payment plan (I think it makes the product more unstable) I use it at work, and until there is a better alternative it will be what I use.

I’ve been playing with Adobe XD, and wow is it fast. I've also been using Sketch a lot more at work, and I'm excited for the potentional of Invision Studio..

For my coding needs I currently use Atom, it’s free and it’s a great app. When I’m working on emails I either use Litmus Builder (great tool) or I use (shudder) Dreamweaver.

For emails I use Litmus for testing, I use Act-On at work, and help my wife with her newsletter at MailChimp.

Other utilities I use most are, Apple Notes to keep all my thoughts organized and connected across all my devices without having to pay monthly. I use Rescue Time to track my productivity level, 1Password for all my passwords, and DayOne for any random thoughts or experiences I want to remember.

I also track my health pretty religiously and use gyrosco.pe app to do that. The apps I use to track my health are, Strava for running, It works great with my Nike+ Apple Watch. I use Withings Body Cardio to track my weight and blood pressure. I use myfitnesspal to track my calorie intake.

For hardware, I use a MacBook Pro. I don’t go anywhere without my iPhone or Apple Watch, and usually my iPad Pro isn’t far away.
